# Felysyum Officially Listed on CoinGecko: A Major Step Forward

We are thrilled to share a significant milestone in our journey — **Felysyum ($FELY)** is now officially listed on [CoinGecko](https://www.coingecko.com/en/coins/felysyum), one of the most trusted and widely used cryptocurrency data platforms in the world.

CoinGecko serves millions of users globally, offering real-time information on token prices, market trends, trading volume, and more. Being listed on such a reputable platform not only brings greater visibility to Felysyum but also boosts credibility and trust among potential investors and partners.

### Why This Matters

*   **Wider Exposure**: Investors, traders, and crypto enthusiasts can now track $FELY easily.
*   **Market Tracking**: Real-time price data, volume, and charts for FELY are now available.
*   **Ecosystem Growth**: Listing on CoinGecko sets the foundation for further listings on exchanges and aggregators like CoinMarketCap and Trust Wallet.
*   **Community Confidence**: This milestone reaffirms our commitment to transparency, legitimacy, and long-term development.

We thank our growing community for their unwavering support, and this is just the beginning. As we continue to expand the Felysyum ecosystem, this listing marks a key step in our mission to make crypto more accessible, usable, and impactful.

Stay tuned — more major announcements are on the horizon!
