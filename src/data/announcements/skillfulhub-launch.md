# SkillfulHub Beta is Here!

We are proud to unveil the beta version of **SkillfulHub**, our decentralized educational platform powered by the Felysyum ecosystem.

### What is SkillfulHub?
SkillfulHub connects learners with expert instructors, offering courses ranging from blockchain technology to digital marketing. By completing courses, users can earn **FET (Feliciamatrix Educational Tokens)** and other rewards.

### Key Features
- **Earn while you learn**: Get rewarded for your progress.
- **Expert Instructors**: Content curated by industry leaders.
- **Community Driven**: Vote on what courses you want to see next.

Visit [SkillfulHub.com](https://skillfulhub.com) to start your learning journey today!
